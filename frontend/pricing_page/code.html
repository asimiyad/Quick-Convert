Knowledge Base

FREQUENTLY ASKED

What files are supported?

Quick Convert supports over 300+ file formats including major document types (PDF, DOCX), images (HEIC, RAW, WEBP), and high-resolution video formats. Our Pro plan includes support for larger archive formats like specialized ZIP and TAR files.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade SSL encryption for all transfers. Files are processed in volatile memory and are permanently wiped from our servers within 1 hour of processing (or immediately if you choose the manual delete option).

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Our subscriptions are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can downgrade to the Student plan at any time through your dashboard settings.

Do you offer discounts?

Our Student plan is already 100% free. For non-profits or large academic institutions looking for Team plans, please contact our sales department for custom high-volume pricing.
